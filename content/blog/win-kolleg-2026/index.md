Today I am participating in a working meeting of the IDEE project within the WIN-Kolleg of the Heidelberger Akademie der Wissenschaften, in cooperation with PD Dr. Prisca Bauer, Dr. Daniel Mandel, and Dr. Mathis Trautwein.

In today's meeting we are preparing the interview protocols for our study on flow experiences and dissociative experiences, and onboarding our student research assistants. The project investigates extreme embodiment experiences from philosophical, psychopathological, and therapeutic perspectives.
